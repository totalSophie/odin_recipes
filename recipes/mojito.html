Mojito

Description

Mix this classic cocktail for a party using fresh mint, white rum, sugar, zesty lime and cooling soda water. Play with the quantities to suit your taste.
Ingredients
  • juice of 1 lime
  • 1 tsp granulated sugar
  • small handful mint leaves, plus extra sprig to serve
  • 60ml white rum
  • soda water, to taste
Method
  1. Muddle the lime juice, sugar and mint leaves in a small jug, crushing the mint as you go
  2. Pour into a tall glass and add a handful of ice.
  3. Top up with soda water, garnish with mint and serve.
